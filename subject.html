About

Valentine's Day or St. Valentine's Day is an annual holiday celebrated of February 14th around the globe. A holiday dedicated to romance and love, families, lovers, friends, and collegues normally spend time together to appreciate their loved ones. They give Valentines greetings and a gift as per custom and exhange affections.

Origins

The origins's of Valentines day are mostly unknown but there are popular theories that the day was meant to celebrate the death of St. Valentine. There are different iterations of who St. Valentine actually is but in all versions, Valentine is a martyr. Other theories suggest it was an effort to "christianize" the holiday 'Lupercalia'.
